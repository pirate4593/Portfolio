About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Joseph O'Connor, a Computer Science Major at UW-Stout with a love for Video Games and how to make them. I thrive on turning ideas into reality through the power of programming.

My journey in programming began in elementary school when I went to a camp that included a segment of making a website. Along the way, I've honed my skills in various programming languages and programs including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java, allowing me to tackle challenges with creativity and precision.

In this portfolio, you'll find a showcase of my projects, each representing a unique blend of innovation, dedication, and problem-solving. From A website that can generate short resumes to A point and click horror game made with Unity, each endeavor reflects my commitment to excellence and my drive to make a meaningful impact.

Feel free to explore and get to know more about my work. I'm excited to connect, collaborate, and bring ideas to life together!
